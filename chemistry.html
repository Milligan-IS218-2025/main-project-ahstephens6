Water Chemistry

Overview

Chemistry may seem to be an extremely complex topic, which it can, however, it can also be made extremely simple. This section will be to talk exclusively about the parameters the water should be kept at for optimal fish and coral health. The parameters I provide are reflective of natural sea water to a degree with some modifications for exaggerated color/growth.
