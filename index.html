Introduction

By: Xander Stephens

This is the final project for my Web Development class. I am going to develop a webpage that explores setting up/owning and taking care of a saltwater fish tank.

This is a somewhat complex hobby that can be broken down into a few major points that I will cover in other parts of this website. I think that this would be a useful guide for anyone that wants to try keeping saltwater aquariums. It could also be used as a cheetsheet for general information regarding aquarium startups.
