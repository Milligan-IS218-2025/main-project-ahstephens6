Coral Overview

General Information

Coral have three main categories: Small Polyp Stony (SPS), Large Polyp Stony (LPS), and Soft coral. Each of these have unique lighting and flow requirements, but generally have the same what chemistry requirements. The following sections will provide lighting arrangements and flow arrangements based on each type of coral. Some coral will differ from the norm and I will link to more information about them when they come up.
