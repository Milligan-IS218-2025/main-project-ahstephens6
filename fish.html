Fish

There are mainly two type of fish you want to look at in keeping saltwater aquariums: reef safe fish and not reef safe fish. Reef safe fish are fish that will not eat coral, and are most likely to not eat other invertebrates. Not reef safe fish are exactly the opposite, they will eat your coral and will most likely also eat your invertebrates. I recommend for smaller tanks, anything under 75 gallons, that people stick with small and peaceful fish to ensure little aggression as well as happiness. Also stick to just a few fish so that you do not over populate your tank. I also don't recommend getting not reef safe fish if you plan on keeping coral.
