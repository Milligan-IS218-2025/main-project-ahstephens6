My Hobby

I have been keeping saltwater aquariums for about 5 years. I keep two tanks at home right now, one is 30 gallons, and the other is 90 gallons. I keep fish, corals, and anemones and have had good success growing and propagating them. This hobby has been a passion of mine for a long time now, and I am excited to explore ways to further my knowledge and advancement of the hobby.
